Machine learning


Machine Learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables computers to learn and improve from experience without explicit programming. It involves algorithms and statistical models that enable systems to recognize patterns, make predictions, and optimize tasks, thereby enhancing their performance in various applications, from data analysis to automation.

After studying Machine Learning, we recommend you to learn Deep Learning concepts.
